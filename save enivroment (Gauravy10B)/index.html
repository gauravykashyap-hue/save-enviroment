What is Environmental Pollution?

Environmental pollution means the contamination of air, water and land due to harmful substances. It affects human health, animals and nature. Protecting the environment is important for a healthy life.

Causes of Environmental Pollution

  • Vehicle smoke
  • Industrial waste
  • Plastic usage
  • Deforestation

Effects of Pollution

  1. Global warming
  2. Health problems
  3. Climate change
  4. Loss of wildlife

Types of Pollution

Type Cause Effect
Air Smoke Breathing problems
Water Waste Water diseases
Land Plastic Soil damage

3R Solutions

Reduce
Use fewer resources and avoid waste.
Reuse
Reuse items instead of throwing them.
Recycle
Recycle waste to save resources.
